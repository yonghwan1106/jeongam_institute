## Generated Card Images

Generated images use a shared visual direction: **정암 기록화 콜라주**. The style combines hanji fiber texture, ink-line drawing, restrained dancheong red/teal/ink tones, gold accents, old field notebook fragments, woodblock texture, and museum-panel composition. Long readable Korean text was intentionally excluded from image prompts; titles and descriptions are rendered in HTML.
